Our Passion & Love

  • Milk/Thai Style

    A refreshing combination of brewed black tea, milk, and (optional) tapioca pearls. Thai is sweetened condensed milk.

  • Taro Bubble Tea

    Incorporates pureed taro, a purple root similar to sweet potato that has a toasty, sweet flavor.

  • Fruit Tea

    A fresh fruit-based tea with your choice of boba that is often caffeine-free. Think of this as a smoothy, but with boba bubbles within it.
