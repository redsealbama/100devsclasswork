What is Boba?

We believe it's the greatest and coolest addition to the world!

There is seriously a TON of different ways to use and apply Boba in Bubble Tea.

Called Tapioca balls officially, boba is edible translucent spheres producted from tapioca. That is a starch extracted from the cassava root. Most commonly refered to as boba or pearls. These boba are typically five to ten millimetres in diameter and you can add differnt ingredients like water, sugar, honey, etc. They can vary in texture and flavor. There are some that even pop! At Bob's we strive to give a variety of differnt options for our customers. If you have a food allergy, please let us know.

Some cool facts!

Believe it or not, boba has been on pizza! It's actually not that bad itself if you were to ask our team members when we recreated it. The possibilities with boba are endless and we always strive to have a ton of fun with our community. If you have an idea, please reach out and let us know!
