About Me

My Story

Hello friends! My name is Jake Davis and I am from a small town outside of Birmingham, Alabama. I am a quadruplet and I am lucky to be with the love of my life Ashley. I began programming years ago whenever I played games like Neopets or Final Fantasy Online. I fell in love with tech very early in my life when I built my first computer and I also really enjoy simplifying things by telling a computer what to do. I want to be able to help others tell their story and promote their business. Albert Einsteins famous quote, "I believe in one thing-that only a life lived for others is a life worth living." has stuck with me for as long as I can remember.

I have met some amazing people in this industry starting with my undergraduate up through graduate school. I am passionate about providing memoriable, innovative and accessible solutions for people with my code. When I am not coding, I can be found spending time with my family, exercising, planning my next destination for travel, playing a video game, and giving a new hobby a whirl (most recently disc golf and the piano).
